Power Pack brings local renewable energy producers and consumers together

Power Pack is a new way to buy renewable energy from people near you at a better price for everyone.

Across the UK, thousands of people have come together to create community renewable solar, wind and hydro energy installations in their local areas.

Their hard work and vision is already saving tonnes of carbon and reducing our dependence on fossil fuels.

These groups are also keeping millions of pounds in their local communities - where it is needed the most.

But many of these groups have a problem. The installations they work so hard to set up only supply a small number of buildings - such as businesses, schools and village halls. These buildings donâ€™t need energy 24/7.

There is no cheap way to store this energy, so the installations are limited. Excess energy sold back to the National Grid at a low price. And there is no cheap way to store this energy.

Meanwhile, households in the local community are paying 2 to 3 times more to buy it back from the National Grid again.

We want to make it easy for community energy groups to connect with people in their area who want to buy their electricity. It's a local energy sharing club with a better price for everyone.

A commitment to Power Pack will lead to a whole new way to generate and sell electricity in the UK.
