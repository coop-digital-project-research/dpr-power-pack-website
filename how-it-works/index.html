Power Pack brings local renewable energy producers and consumers together

Power Pack is a new way for communities to buy renewable energy from local producers at a better price for everyone.

More and more people are putting solar panels and wind turbines on their homes, businesses and community buildings. But renewable energy generation is variable.

Often the solar panels and turbines make more energy than the people who own them can use. And there is no cheap way to store this energy.

So they sell it back to to the National Grid at a low price. Meanwhile their neighbours are paying 2 to 3 times more to buy it back off the National Grid again.

Power Pack makes it easy to buy excess energy from your neighbours. It's a local energy sharing club with a better price for everyone.

A commitment to Power Pack will lead to a whole new way to generate and sell electricity in the UK.

Join Power Pack and get more of your electricity from local renewable sources.

When it's available, you'll use electricity from local wind turbines and solar panels - at a better price for everyone.
