Raise money by switching households to green power

Fundraise for your school without having to ask for money

All you have to do is to ask families to switch to a green energy supplier — we'll take care of everything else. We'll print personalised leaflets for school bags and letters and emails.

Have a look at this example fundraising page.

What you get

  • £25 for every household that switches to a green energy company
  • Ready made, printed materials to distribute to schools (either letters for bags, or emails to send)
  • A web page for your school to promote it's fundraising campaign
  • Regular reports on how many households have switched, how much money you’ve raised, and how much CO2 you've saved
  • Template tweets / Facebook posts / emails that you can send as follow ups to families keeping them up to date, and encouraging others to switch

How it works

  • You’re not asking households for money — you’re asking them to switch energy supplier.
  • We find the best deal for the household — from a 100% renewable energy company.
  • If they’ve never switched before — they will save money.
