We are the digital product research team at Co-op Digital in Manchester.

We've created Power Pack to bring people together to buy and sell local renewable energy.

Before we start making software, we need to get our feet muddy. Understanding how relationships in a community like this will work means getting a bunch of people together in a field. Our approach is to learn by doing - getting out of the office and into the real world.

Power Pack is inspired by Energy Local's work at Bethesda and Shrivenham.
